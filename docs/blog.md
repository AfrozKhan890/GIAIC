### Paper of the Month: "Neuromorphic Control for Energy-Efficient Locomotion"
*Journal: Science Robotics | March 2025*

**Summary**: Researchers at NeuroRobotics Lab have developed a neuromorphic controller that reduces energy consumption in bipedal robots by 40% compared to traditional approaches. The system uses spiking neural networks implemented on specialized hardware.

**Key Contributions:**
- Novel event-based control architecture
- Hardware-software co-design approach
- Validation on multiple robot platforms
- Open-source implementation available

**Relevance to Textbook**: Connects to Chapters 4 (Neural Control) and 8 (Edge AI)

**Link**: [Read Paper](https://robotics.sciencemag.org/neuromorphic-control)
