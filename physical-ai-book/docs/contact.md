## Connect With Us

Building Physical AI is a collaborative journey. Whether you're seeking help, wanting to contribute, or looking to connect with fellow learners and practitioners, there are many ways to engage with our growing community.

## Community Platforms

### Discord Community
**Primary communication channel** for real-time discussion and support.

**Channels Available:**
- `#general`: Community introductions and announcements
- `#help`: Technical questions and problem-solving
- `#projects`: Showcase and discuss your work
- `#research`: Latest papers and discoveries
- `#career`: Job opportunities and career advice
- `#local-meetups`: Organize in-person events

**Join Link**: [Discord Invite](https://discord.gg/physical-ai-community)

**Community Guidelines**:
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Help others when you can
- Share knowledge generously
- Keep discussions constructive
- Respect confidentiality of shared projects

### GitHub Organization
**Code collaboration and issue tracking** for the textbook and related projects.

**Main Repositories**:
- [Textbook Source](https://github.com/AfrozKhan890/physical-ai-book): Complete textbook code and content
- [Example Projects](https://github.com/PhysicalAI-Examples): Starter code and solutions
- [Community Projects](https://github.com/PhysicalAI-Community): User-contributed projects
- [Tools and Utilities](https://github.com/PhysicalAI-Tools): Helper scripts and packages

**How to Contribute**:
1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a pull request
5. Participate in code review

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](https://github.com/AfrozKhan890/physical-ai-book/CONTRIBUTING.md) for detailed guidelines.

### Discussion Forum
**For longer-form discussions** and knowledge sharing.

**Forum Categories**:
- **Getting Started**: Installation help and beginner questions
- **Technical Deep Dives**: Advanced topics and implementation details
- **Project Feedback**: Get input on your work
- **Education Resources**: Teaching materials and curriculum ideas
- **Research Collaboration**: Find partners for projects

**Forum Link**: [Physical AI Forum](https://forum.physical-ai.edu)

**Moderation**: Community-moderated with volunteer facilitators
